Just spread awareness. A lot of people see things like "flex fuel" on fuel pumps and think nothing of it. If people were aware of how much potential it could have to substantially reduce the amount of greenhouse gas emitted, we would be in a much better situation. If we mandated something like this for all cars that came out, that would give us the time needed to get our infrastructure in order. This new energy conservation could offset any harmful environmental factors that we could potentially introduce while updating our infrastructure.
