We need change

The government thought it was a brilliant idea to continue to mine ultra rare metals. But if the planet is already as far gone as it is, Why don't we just use what we have?

Flex Fuel

One thing we already have is 'FlexFuel'. This fuel source has already proven itself effective in many applications.

What are the benefits?

Flex fuel can literally be added on to any efi (electronic fuel injection) vehicle and the fuel itself burns up to 50% cleaner than gasoline.

What's the catch?


Though they are minuscule, there are a couple downsides involving flexfuel as a whole. Arguably the benefits far outweigh the caveats.
